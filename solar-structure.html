SALASAR Module Mounting Structures are designed and engineered for each customer's site specific conditions to minimize the field installation labor and require no filed welding, drilling or other on-site fabrication.
SALASAR MMS is manufactured under controlled conditions, in "State-of-the Art" manufacturing systems, adhering to safety and superior quality parameters.

SALASAR-C and SALASAR-Z PURLINS

are secondary members of steel structures which serve as the basic material of construction for fast-track projects. These purlins are categorized by high strength, yet low cost (as a result of the high strength to weight ratio). Purlins are supplied in required sizes and lengths with pre-punched holes for quick bolting. The production system deployed consists of international standard cold-roll forming technology using high quality machines with online measuring, cutting, punching and forming to provide assured dimensions and straightness, and an overall supreme quality product.

Advantages

  • 1. Very high strength-to—weight ratio: high strength – low cost
  • 2. More than 40% saving in steel
  • 3. More than 30% saving in overall construction cost
  • 4. Easy and low-cost handling and transportation
  • 5. Economy due to reduction in dead weight on the main frame structure
  • 6. Significantly reduce installation time
  • 7. Ability to span long lengths
  • 8. No site drilling or cutting required
  • 9. Maintenance free owing to application of proper surface finish
  • 10. Purlins are factory finished, cut to application of proper surface finish
  • 11. Continuous splay and splicing for better structural strength, stability and economy
  • 12. 45 degree(use degree sign) lip for better sectional modulus and easy nesting

Technical & Material Specification

  • Thickness: 1.5mm to3mm
  • Length: Upto 12 METRE
  • Material: CR Steel as per IS:513/ HR Steel as per IS: 1079
  • Yield Strength: 250 MPa to 350 MPa



Features:

  • 1. Suitable for flat and slightly sloped roofs
  • 2. Suitable for several module dimensions with a panel width from 950 to 1050mm
  • 3. Total dead load of panels with steel structure and ballast below 10kg/m² of roof achievable
  • 4. Standard and angle regulation from 5⁰C TO 30⁰C Z and C Purlins / C Channels, bracings, angles and column post are members of Module Mounting structure. They are rolled formed bu using pre-galvanized, post galvanized or made of galvano steel.

Pre galvanized structure

  • 1. Thickness: 0.8 to 3 mm
  • 2. Material Yielding Sheet: 250 Mpa - 350 Mpa
  • 3. Line Coating: 120 – 550 GSM
  • 4. Material confirming to IS:277


Post galvanized structure

  • 1. Material Yielding Sheet: 250 Mpa - 350 Mpa
  • 2. Hot dip galvanization up to 85 microns/ 550 GSM
  • 3. Hot dip galvanization confirms IS:2629/ ASTM A153

Galvano Steel

  • 1. Thickness 0.8 - 1.2 mm
  • 2. Material Yielding Sheet: 250 Mpa - 550 Mpa
  • 3. Aluminum Zing Coating: AZ - 70 / AZ – 150
  • 4. Material confirms to ASTM, A792, SS80
