HELICAL SCREW PILES, EXTENSIONS AND IT'S ACCESSORIES

We manufacture and offers a vide range of superior quality helical screw piles, extensions and its accessories. These are manufactured as per our client specifications & design. SE offers both bare and galvanised finished product in a wide variety of sizes to suit any foundation application.

  1. -- Corrosion Protection to conform to ASTM A153 Standard – Hot Dip Galvanizing
  2. -- Seamless Tubes as per ASTM A 106 Grade B A500
  3. -- Solid Steel Bar (RCS) per ASTM A29 with 90ksi minimum yield strength
  4. -- Bolt & Nuts as per SAE J429 Grade 8.
  5. Helix Plate: ASTM A36 or as per Indian Standard IS: 2062 Grade (E350)
  6. -- T BRACKETS, JACKING PLATE & BRACKETS are manufactured as per customer’s requirements.

Screwpiles, sometimes referred to as screw piles and also called Helical piles, are a steel screw-in piling and ground anchoring system used for building deep foundations. Screwpiles are manufactured using varying sizes of tubular hollow sections for the pile or anchors shaft.
The pile shaft transfers a structure's load into the pile. Helical steel plates are welded to the pile shaft in accordance with the intended ground conditions. Helicals can be press-formed to a specified pitch or simply consist of flat plates welded at a specified pitch to the pile's shaft. The number of helices, their diameters and position on the pile shaft as well as steel plate thickness are all determined by a combination of:

  1. 1. The combined structure design load requirement.
  2. 2. The geotechnical parameters.
  3. 3. Environmental corrosion parameters.
  4. 4. The minimum design life of the structure being supported or restrained.
