STEEL ACCESS COVER & FRAMES

Salasar has more than 18 years of experience in designing and manufacturing fabricated steel access covers and frames. We manufacture a wide range of steel access covers & frames from pedestrian to heavy duty steel covers. Our range includes:

  1. 1. Block Paviour
  2. 2. Solid top steel access covers.
        a> Pressed
        b> Chequered
  3. 3. Recessed trays.

BLOCK PAVIOR

Block paviour steel access covers eliminates the need to cut paving around the manholes and maintain a uniform pattern in the driveway and pavments. These covers are galvanised and can withstand huge weights as per the design. The table shows the standard sizes we manufacture. SOLID TOP STEEL ACCESS COVER & FRAME

Suitable for pedestrians and slow moving traffic, these covers provide a cost effective solution for manhole covers. The surface is designed in such a way that it avoids water pooling. Further, the surface seals negate the possibility of water seepage and odors. We manufacture Pressed covers, generally used for domestic installations and Chequered covers, generally used for industrial installations.
