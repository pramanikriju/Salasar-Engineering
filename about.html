About us.

About Salasar

Founded in 2013, Salasar Engineering, a part of the JIYA Group, was incorporated to become one of the leading manufacturers of steel fabricated products and assemblies. Led by Mr. Vikash Agarwala, SE continues to stand by its strong principles of customer satisfaction.
The JIYA Group was founded in 1994 with a sole motive of serving the global market with quality products. In 2013, JIYA group diversified to Steel fabrication products. Salasar Engineering started with local clients of Eastern India to understand the customer’s perspective and gain rich experience. Gradually, with proper infrastructure and certifications, SE started catering to the global market. SE maintains highest quality standards. Our group of highly motivated and expert technicians strives to provide the highest quality products at cost effective prices.
